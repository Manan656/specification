Apricus Heat Pump: Advanced, Eco Friendly Hot Water Systems


The Apricus Heat Pump 260L is a cutting-edge system designed for efficient hot water heating. By utilizing renewable energy from the air, this heat pump significantly reduces electricity consumption compared to traditional electric water heaters, making it an ideal choice for environmentally conscious homeowners.


Benefits of the Apricus Heat Pump


Exceptional Energy Efficiency: The Apricus all in one heat pump can lower energy costs by up to 70%. Its advanced technology extracts heat from ambient air—even in lower temperatures—ensuring substantial savings on energy bills.

Environmentally Sustainable: By leveraging renewable energy sources, the Apricus hot water systems contributes to a reduction in greenhouse gas emissions, promoting sustainability and enhancing your home's eco-friendly profile.

Low Maintenance: This heat pump requires minimal upkeep due to its durable construction and fewer moving parts. Regular servicing is recommended, but overall, it offers hassle-free operation for years.

Versatile Installation: The compact design of the Apricus all in one heat pump allows for flexible installation in various settings, making it suitable for new builds or retrofits without extensive renovations.



How It Works Hot Water Heat Pumps:

The Apricus Heat Pump uses a refrigerant to absorb heat from the air, compresses it, and transfers it to the water in the tank. This efficient operation ensures a reliable hot water supply, even in cooler climates.
